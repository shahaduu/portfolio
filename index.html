Featured Projects

Health Armor

AI-Powered Wearable Healthcare System

Health Armor is an intelligent wearable healthcare system designed to predict the risk of Sudden Infant Death Syndrome (SIDS) through continuous monitoring of infants' physiological indicators. The system collects real-time health data including heart rate, oxygen saturation (SpO₂), body temperature, and body movement, then applies Artificial Intelligence algorithms to detect abnormal patterns and generate early alerts for caregivers. The project aims to improve infant safety by providing early prediction and continuous monitoring through wearable technology and intelligent healthcare solutions.

Key Features

  • Real-time health monitoring.
  • SIDS risk prediction.
  • Heart rate monitoring.
  • Blood oxygen (SpO₂) monitoring.
  • Body temperature monitoring.
  • Movement analysis.
  • AI-based prediction.
  • Early warning notifications.

Technologies: Artificial Intelligence • Machine Learning • IoT • ESP32 • Biomedical Sensors • Python
